Attributes

  • width - Integer corresponding to the emote's width in pixels

  • speed - Floating point number corresponding to the factor multiplied by the emote's regular speed
  • x - Integer corresponding to the emote's x position if positioned absolutely
  • y - Integer corresponding to the emote's y position if positioned absolutely
