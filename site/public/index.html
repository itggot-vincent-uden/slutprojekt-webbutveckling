Emotes

All emotes from Better Twitch.Tv are availible at any time through our servers via API-requests. This includes all global and channel specific image and gif emotes such as Coggers and Feelsgoodman.

Performance

We guarantee the best performance through our multitude of servers which are always up no matter the time of day. Emotes are also availible for caching if you don't feel like using the internet.
